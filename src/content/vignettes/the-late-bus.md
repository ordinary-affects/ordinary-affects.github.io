The bus is late and the people at the stop arrange themselves into a loose constellation of waiting. A phone comes out, goes back in, comes out again. Someone shifts their weight. Nobody says the thing everyone is thinking, which is only that time has come slightly loose from its track and will, in a minute, click back into place.
